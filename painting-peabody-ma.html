Exterior Painting in Peabody, MA

Professional residential and commercial painting — 8 miles from our Wakefield home base

Peabody covers a lot of ground — from the downtown Peabody Square area with its older commercial buildings and tightly packed residential streets to the sprawling suburban neighborhoods of West Peabody where colonials and raised ranches sit on half-acre lots. Paul's Painting serves the entire 01960 zip code, bringing the same preparation-first approach whether we are painting a storefront on Main Street or a four-bedroom colonial off Route 114.

The commercial corridor along Route 1 and Route 114 keeps us busy with retail facades, office parks, and restaurant exteriors that need to look sharp and stand up to heavy traffic exposure. Downtown Peabody Square has its own character — brick and wood-frame buildings from the city's leather industry days that require careful surface prep before painting. We power wash, scrape peeling coatings, repair rotted trim, prime bare wood, and caulk every gap before finish coats go on. That level of prep is what separates a paint job that lasts five to seven years from one that starts peeling after the first winter.

West Peabody homeowners tend to have larger properties with more exterior surface area — attached garages, farmer's porches, multiple gable ends, and extensive trim. We handle the scale without cutting corners. Every surface gets the attention it needs, from the peak of the gable to the foundation line. We use Sherwin-Williams exterior paints and stains chosen for the specific substrate and exposure, and we leave the job site cleaner than we found it.
