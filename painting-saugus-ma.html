Exterior Painting in Saugus, MA

Residential and commercial painting — 5 miles from our Wakefield home base

Saugus is a town of contrasts, and that variety keeps our crews busy. Along the Route 1 corridor, commercial properties — from retail storefronts to office buildings — need exterior coatings that hold up against road grime, salt spray, and constant sun exposure. We handle the prep and scheduling that commercial work demands, coordinating with property managers and business owners to minimize disruption during operating hours.

Off the main corridors, Saugus has solid residential neighborhoods filled with ranch-style homes, split-levels, and capes built through the 1950s, 60s, and 70s. Many of these houses sit on the streets branching off Lincoln Avenue and throughout the neighborhoods near Breakheart Reservation. These homes often have original wood trim, aging clapboard, or aluminum siding that could use fresh paint and proper prep work. We scrape, sand, prime bare wood, and caulk every seam before the finish coat goes on — the kind of groundwork that separates a paint job that peels in two years from one that lasts a decade.

Being only five miles from our Wakefield base means we can get crews to Saugus quickly, whether it is a single-family ranch on Vine Street or a multi-unit commercial building along Route 1. We use Sherwin-Williams Duration and SuperPaint for their proven performance in the 01906 area's exposure to coastal winds and New England freeze-thaw cycles.
