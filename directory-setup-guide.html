1. Google Business Profile

Highest Priority
google.com/business
  1. Go to google.com/business and click "Manage now"
  2. Sign in with a Google account (or create one for the business)
  3. Enter business name: Paul's Painting
  4. Select primary category: Painter
  5. Add secondary category: Painting contractor
  6. Choose "Yes" for serving customers at their location
  7. Enter business address: Butternut Road, Wakefield, MA 01880
  8. Add service area -- enter all 12 towns: Wakefield, Melrose, Stoneham, Reading, Saugus, Lynnfield, Woburn, Winchester, Medford, Malden, Peabody, North Reading
  9. Enter phone number: 781-254-1192
  10. Enter website: https://pauls-painting.com
  11. Set hours: Mon-Sat 7:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Sunday closed
  12. Add a business description mentioning exterior painting, Wakefield, and the North Shore
  13. Upload photos: job photos, before-and-after shots, team at work
  14. Choose a verification method -- Google will offer postcard, phone, or email
  15. Complete verification (postcard takes 5-7 days)

After verification: Post weekly updates with job photos. Respond to every review (positive or negative) within 24 hours. Add new photos regularly. This is the single biggest driver of local search visibility.

2. Yelp Business

High Priority
biz.yelp.com
  1. Go to biz.yelp.com
  2. Search for "Paul's Painting Wakefield MA" to see if a listing already exists
  3. If it exists, click "Claim this business" and follow the steps
  4. If not, click "Add your business" and create a new listing
  5. Fill out all business information (name, address, phone, website, hours)
  6. Select category: Painters
  7. Upload at least 5 photos -- completed jobs, the team, close-ups of detail work
  8. Write a business description highlighting services and service area
  9. Verify ownership via phone or email

Important: Never ask customers to leave a Yelp review directly -- Yelp's filter will hide them. Instead, just do great work. Respond to every review professionally. Yelp offers paid advertising but the free listing is enough to start.

3. Angi (formerly Angie's List)

Medium Priority
angi.com/pro
  1. Go to angi.com/pro
  2. Click "Join as a Pro" or "Create Account"
  3. Enter business name, contact info, and service area
  4. Select services: Exterior Painting, House Painting
  5. Upload business license or insurance documentation if prompted
  6. Add photos and a detailed business description
  7. Complete the profile -- fill out every field for best visibility

Cost: Basic listing is free. Angi offers paid lead generation and advertising, but start with the free profile and see what comes in organically.

4. HomeAdvisor

Medium Priority
homeadvisor.com/pro
  1. Go to homeadvisor.com/pro
  2. Click "Sign Up" to create a pro account
  3. Enter business name, phone, and zip code (01880)
  4. Select trade: Painting
  5. Fill out the full profile with description, photos, and service details
  6. Set your service area to the 12 towns
  7. Complete background check if required

Cost warning: HomeAdvisor charges per lead, typically $15-$50 per lead depending on the project. Start with just the free profile. Only turn on paid leads if you want to test it, and set a strict weekly budget cap so costs do not run away.

5. Thumbtack

Medium Priority
thumbtack.com/pro
  1. Go to thumbtack.com/pro
  2. Click "Join as a pro"
  3. Enter your name, email, and zip code
  4. Select service: House Painting (then refine to Exterior Painting)
  5. Set your service area -- enter each of the 12 towns or set a radius from Wakefield
  6. Set your availability and travel preferences
  7. Add photos, a description, and any credentials
  8. Set a weekly budget for leads (you choose the amount)

Cost: Pay-per-lead model. You set a weekly budget and Thumbtack sends leads until it is spent. Start low ($50-$100/week) to test the quality of leads in your area. You can pause or stop at any time.

6. Facebook Business Page

Medium Priority
facebook.com/pages/create
  1. Log in to Facebook with a personal account
  2. Go to facebook.com/pages/create
  3. Choose category: Local Business
  4. Enter business name: Paul's Painting
  5. Select subcategory: Painter
  6. Add address, phone number, and website
  7. Set hours: Mon-Sat 7:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  8. Upload a profile photo (logo or team photo) and cover photo (a completed job)
  9. Write an "About" section with a business description
  10. Enable the Reviews tab in page settings
  11. Post your first update with a job photo

Ongoing: Post job photos regularly (1-2 per week). Before-and-after photos perform especially well. Respond to messages and reviews promptly. A Facebook page also shows up in Google search results.

7. Nextdoor Business

Medium Priority
business.nextdoor.com
  1. Go to business.nextdoor.com
  2. Click "Claim your free business page"
  3. Search for your business or create a new listing
  4. Enter business name, address, phone, and website
  5. Select category: Painter
  6. Add a description and photos
  7. Verify your business

Why it matters: Nextdoor is where neighbors recommend local businesses to each other. When someone asks "anyone know a good painter?" on Nextdoor, your business page can come up. Free listing gives local visibility that is hard to get elsewhere.

8. BuildZoom

Lower Priority
buildzoom.com
  1. Go to buildzoom.com
  2. Search for "Paul's Painting" to see if a profile already exists from public records
  3. If found, click "Claim this profile"
  4. If not, look for "Add your business" or "Join as a contractor"
  5. Fill out all available fields: business info, service area, specialties
  6. Upload photos if the option is available
  7. Complete verification steps

Cost: Free listing. BuildZoom pulls data from permit records and license databases. Having a claimed, complete profile helps with online presence and can show up in search results.
