Exterior Painting in Lynnfield, MA

Premium residential painting — 4 miles from our Wakefield home base

Lynnfield homes tend to be larger, set back from the road on generous wooded lots, and built with the kind of architectural detail that demands a painter who pays attention. Multi-story colonials, gambrels, and custom-built homes are the norm here — properties where cutting corners on prep work shows immediately. We bring the ladders, staging, and crew size to handle two- and three-story exteriors safely and efficiently, without rushing the work that matters most: scraping old paint, priming bare wood, and caulking every joint before the first finish coat goes on.

Near the MarketStreet Lynnfield area, newer construction and mixed-use properties require a different approach. These buildings often feature composite trim, fiber-cement siding, and modern coatings that need specific primers and application techniques. We stay current on manufacturer requirements — painting HardiePlank wrong voids the warranty, and homeowners in the 01940 zip code are not the type to accept that kind of mistake. We do the homework so the finish coat bonds properly and looks right for years.

The detailed trim work common on Lynnfield homes — window casings, crown molding, decorative brackets, and multi-pane sash windows — takes patience. Our crews brush trim by hand rather than spraying everything, because brush work lays down a smoother, more durable finish on these smaller surfaces. We mask carefully, cut clean lines, and leave the property looking sharp. Sherwin-Williams Duration Exterior in a satin or semi-gloss finish is our go-to for Lynnfield trim, offering excellent adhesion and the kind of sheen that holds up to New England's rain, snow, and UV exposure.
