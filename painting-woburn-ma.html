Exterior Painting in Woburn, MA

Residential and commercial painting — 6 miles from our Wakefield home base

Woburn covers a lot of ground, from quiet residential streets to the busy commercial corridor along Route 128 and I-93. That mix of property types is something we handle regularly. On the commercial side, office parks, retail buildings, and industrial properties near the interstate need exterior coatings that look professional and stand up to heavy traffic exposure and road salt. We work with property managers and building owners throughout the 01801 and 01888 zip codes to schedule painting around tenants and business operations.

The residential neighborhoods in Woburn are full of older homes — capes, colonials, and bungalows built from the 1940s through the 1970s — that often need more than just a fresh coat of paint. Peeling clapboard, cracked trim, and chalking surfaces are common on homes that have gone too long between paint jobs. We start every Woburn project with a thorough assessment: power washing the siding, scraping any loose or flaking paint down to a sound surface, sanding rough spots, priming all bare wood, and caulking gaps around windows and door frames. That groundwork is what makes the difference between a paint job that starts peeling after one winter and one that holds for eight to ten years.

We also work with several condo complexes and multi-unit buildings in Woburn. These projects require coordination with HOA boards and property management companies — phased scheduling, color approval processes, and keeping common areas accessible during the work. Near Horn Pond, the tree-shaded properties deal with extra moisture and mildew growth that needs to be killed and cleaned before painting. We treat those surfaces with a mildewcide wash as part of our standard prep, not as an up-charge.
