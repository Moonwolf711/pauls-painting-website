Exterior Painting in Medford, MA

Professional residential and commercial painting — 8 miles from our Wakefield home base

Medford is one of the most architecturally diverse cities north of Boston. From the classic triple-deckers packed along Salem Street and South Medford to the stately Victorians near Tufts University, every block presents a different set of surfaces, substrates, and challenges. Paul's Painting has worked on properties throughout Medford's 02155 and 02156 zip codes, handling everything from three-story multi-family homes to single-family colonials along the Mystic River.

Multi-family homes require more than just a good coat of paint. They require coordination — working around multiple tenants, managing scaffolding on narrow lots, and dealing with decades of layered paint that needs proper scraping and priming before anything new goes on. In Medford Square and the surrounding neighborhoods, we regularly work on buildings where the previous paint job was done poorly, leaving peeling clapboards, exposed wood, and water damage that has to be addressed before the first brush stroke. We handle all of that prep work ourselves.

Medford's condo associations and property managers also call on us for commercial exterior work — storefronts near Medford Square, office buildings, and HOA-managed complexes where the specs are strict and the timeline matters. We use Sherwin-Williams paints rated for New England weather, and every project gets thorough power washing, scraping, caulking, and priming before finish coats go on.

Why Medford Property Owners Choose Paul's Painting

  • Multi-family experience. We understand the logistics of painting triple-deckers and condos — working around tenants, managing tight lot lines, and handling three-story scaffolding safely.
  • Proper prep on old buildings. Medford has a lot of homes built before 1950. We know how to deal with lead paint, failing wood, and layers of old coatings that need to come off before new paint will hold.
  • Clean, professional job sites. In a dense city like Medford, your neighbors are close. We keep our work areas organized, protect landscaping and walkways, and leave the property looking better than we found it.
  • Straight pricing, no surprises. You get a detailed written estimate before we start. If we find additional issues during prep, we discuss them with you before proceeding.
