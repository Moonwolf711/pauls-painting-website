Exterior Painting in Stoneham, MA

4 miles from Wakefield — professional exterior painting for Stoneham homes and businesses

Stoneham sits right next door to our Wakefield base, and we have been painting homes here for years. The neighborhoods near Spot Pond and the Middlesex Fells Reservation are full of well-kept split-levels and ranches from the 1950s and 60s — homes where the original paint or siding is often due for a serious refresh. These properties have their own prep requirements: aluminum siding that needs deoxidizing, wood trim that has taken decades of moisture from the surrounding woods, and concrete foundations that show their age. We handle all of it.

Closer to Stoneham's center, the housing shifts toward classic colonials and cape-style homes along the streets near Town Common and the Main Street corridor. Many of these homes have layered-on paint from multiple owners over the decades. Proper prep on these projects means stripping back to stable surfaces, priming bare wood, and filling every crack and nail hole before the first topcoat. We do not paint over problems — we fix them first. It takes longer, but the finish lasts years longer because of it.

We also serve commercial properties along Main Street, Route 28, and the business district around Montvale Avenue. Stoneham business owners get the same level of professionalism as residential customers: accurate estimates, clear scheduling, and clean work that reflects well on their business. Every project — residential or commercial — uses Sherwin-Williams paints selected for the specific surface and exposure conditions.
