Exterior Painting in Melrose, MA

Just 3 miles from our Wakefield home base — fast response, no travel delays

Melrose is one of the most rewarding towns to paint in. Its tree-lined streets are packed with well-maintained historic homes, and homeowners here genuinely care about curb appeal. We have painted colonials and Victorians along Upham Street, refreshed multi-family homes in the Highlands, and taken on full exterior projects in the Wyoming neighborhood near the Stoneham border. Every Melrose project gets the same thorough process: detailed inspection, honest estimate, and paint work that holds up for years.

The housing in Melrose covers a wide range — grand Victorians with wraparound porches and ornamental trim near Ell Pond, classic New England colonials on the streets off Main Street, and tighter-lot bungalows and duplexes throughout the east side of town. Each style brings different prep challenges. Victorian trim needs careful scraping and priming to avoid peeling. Colonials with clapboard siding need every seam caulked before a single coat goes on. We adapt our approach to the house, not the other way around.

Melrose's downtown along Main Street is also home to shops, restaurants, and small businesses that depend on a clean, professional exterior. We work with business owners to schedule painting around operating hours and minimize disruption. Whether your property faces the Melrose Common or sits on a quiet residential side street, Paul's Painting brings the same attention to detail — Sherwin-Williams paints, thorough surface prep, and a clean site when we pack up.
