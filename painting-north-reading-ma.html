Exterior Painting in North Reading, MA

Professional residential painting — 7 miles from our Wakefield home base

North Reading is a quiet, residential town where the homes range from modest capes built in the 1950s and 60s to large colonials and custom builds on wooded lots along the Ipswich River. The 01864 zip code has a distinctly suburban feel — bigger yards, more trees, and properties where the exterior of the house is a major part of the curb appeal. Paul's Painting serves North Reading homeowners who want their homes painted properly the first time, with prep work that does not get skipped and materials that hold up to the heavy shade and moisture that come with living among mature trees.

Many North Reading homes sit on larger lots with long driveways and setbacks from the road. That means more exterior surface area — wrap-around porches, attached garages, breezeway connections, shed dormers, and extensive trim that all need to be prepped and painted as part of the job. We work through each section methodically, scraping loose paint, sanding rough spots, filling nail holes, caulking gaps around windows and trim boards, and priming bare wood before any finish coat goes on. For homes near the Ipswich River or in low-lying areas where moisture is a persistent problem, we select paints and primers designed to resist mildew and moisture penetration.

North Reading does not have the dense commercial districts you find in neighboring cities, but we do handle the occasional small business, church, or municipal building that needs exterior painting. The work is the same regardless of the building type: thorough surface preparation, quality Sherwin-Williams products, and a clean site when we pack up. We treat every North Reading property with the same respect we would give our own home.
