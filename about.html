About Paul's Painting

Locally owned, quality craftsmanship, Butternut Road, Wakefield

Meet Paul Yianacopolus

Paul Yianacopolus has been delivering quality exterior painting services to homeowners and businesses throughout the Wakefield, Massachusetts area. Based on Butternut Road in Wakefield, Paul's Painting is a locally owned and operated business built on hard work, honest pricing, and craftsmanship you can see.

Whether it's refreshing the exterior of a classic New England colonial, painting a commercial storefront, or helping you pick the perfect color for your home's curb appeal — Paul and his team handle every project with care and professionalism.

We use premium Sherwin-Williams paints, prepare every surface meticulously, and stand behind our work. When you hire Paul's Painting, you're hiring a neighbor who takes pride in making your property look its best.

Your Painting Journey

Here's exactly what happens from first call to finished project. Click any step to learn more.

1

Free Estimate Visit

▼
Day 1 — 30-45 minutes

Paul visits your property, measures surfaces, inspects condition, discusses your color preferences, and answers all your questions. No pressure, no obligation.

  • Measure all paintable surfaces
  • Inspect for rot, peeling, caulking needs
  • Discuss color options and finishes
  • Review timeline and scheduling
2

Written Proposal

▼
Within 2-3 days

You receive a detailed, itemized proposal with exact pricing — no surprises.

  • Itemized pricing — know exactly what you're paying for
  • Specific Sherwin-Williams colors and product lines
  • Estimated start date and project duration
  • Clear warranty terms
3

Scheduling & Color Lock

▼
You choose your date

Once you approve the proposal, we lock in your schedule and finalize colors.

  • Pick a start date that works for you
  • Final color confirmation (samples available)
  • Weather monitoring begins
  • Materials ordered and staged
4

Surface Preparation

▼
Project Day 1-2

This is where the magic starts. Proper prep is 80% of a lasting paint job.

  • Power wash all surfaces
  • Scrape and sand loose or peeling paint
  • Repair wood rot, caulk gaps and seams
  • Mask windows, protect landscaping
  • Apply primer to bare surfaces
5

Painting

▼
Project Day 2-4

Two coats of premium Sherwin-Williams paint applied with care.

  • First coat — full coverage application
  • Dry time between coats (weather dependent)
  • Second coat — final finish quality
  • Trim, detail, and accent work
6

Detail & Touch-Up

▼
Project Day 4-5

We go over everything with fresh eyes. Every drip, edge, and transition gets inspected and perfected.

  • Close-up inspection of all surfaces
  • Touch-up any imperfections
  • Clean edges and cut-in lines
  • Remove all masking and drop cloths
7

Walk-Through & Cleanup

▼
Final Day

You walk the entire project with Paul. If anything isn't perfect, we fix it on the spot.

  • Full walk-through with you
  • Address any concerns immediately
  • Complete site cleanup
  • Landscaping protection removed
✔

Warranty & Paint Record

▼
Ongoing

Your warranty activates and we provide a complete record of every color and product used.

  • Written workmanship warranty
  • Digital paint record (colors, products, dates)
  • Touch-up paint left on-site
  • We're always just a call away
